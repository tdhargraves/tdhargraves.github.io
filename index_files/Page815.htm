Cypress Point Strata

Strata Council Meeting Minutes

The council (seven volunteer owners) and our administrator meet, on average, every month. These minutes are summaries of our meetings.

We strive to ensure our bylaws are being followed by all, for the benefit of all.

Please note that these internet versions of our minutes are password protected and printing is disabled. Owners have knowledge of the passwords, and are welcome to browse. Paper copies of the minutes can be purchased from the Cypress Point Office.
